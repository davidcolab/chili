What you'll need:
2 pounds of ground meat
30 oz diced mangoes
28 oz diced tomatoes
28 oz crushed tomatoes
1 diced sweet or yellow onion
1 diced bell pepper
1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
32 oz dark red kidney beans

Optional ingredients:
diced jalapenos
ground cayenne pepper
shredde cheese (for serving)

Directions:
1. Brown ground meat in a skillet. Drain off excess grease and put meat in a large pot.
2. Add tomatoes, onions, bell peppers, mangoes, and chocolate chips. Save some diced onions for later.
3. Add 4-6 tablespoons of the chili mix. Stir well and taste. Add diced jalapenos or ground cayenne pepper to desired heat.
4. Let simmer for 1-2 hours and stir occasionally. Taste often.
5. Shortly before serving, add in the kidney beans.
6. Serve with diced onions and shredded cheese.
